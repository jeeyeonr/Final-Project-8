User Persona

A persona is a representation of a fictitious user that includes a concise summary of characteristics of the user, their experience, goals and tasks, pain points, and environmental conditions. Personas describe the target users of a tool, site, product or application, giving a clear picture of how they're likely to use the system, and what theyâ€™ll expect from it.

User Persona Benefits

If you do your research property, the persona gives you the chance of engaging with the clients and keeps the clients focused on the UX studies.

Persona enables to collect the painpoints, needs and background information from users. These collected data from different user can become an opportunity for the firms to increase their services based upon the user personas.

Knowing the audience through constructing user persona will enable to create better marketing strategies & corporate strategies.
Several Side Effects of User Persona

Creating the user persona can narrow down the user group since it is very hard to get all the data of users through personas. So this difficulties & limitation of collecting user persona will lead to the narrow scope of the audience group and can create a biased result.

Learner personas make it easier for the employees to distance themselves from the clients.

If your teams does not put in the effort to do the research for the learner personas, you may not receive the desired results.
